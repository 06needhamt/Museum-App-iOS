Planetarium

A trip to the free planetarium is a must for all visitors to the museum! You can blast off on a spectacular journey through space and time, learning about the Universe on your way.You will be able to get up-close to the moon or the volcanoes of Mars from the comfort of your seat.

Entrance to the planetarium is free. Tickets are available to get from the information desk on the day of the show from 10am until 5 minutes before the show starts.Tickets cannot be pre-booked in advance.
