How to use the application

Quiz trail - Take a trail around each exhibit, while learning at your own pace

Explorer Trail - follow a trail around exhibits, while learning at your own pace

Code Scanner - Scan any QR code around the museum to view more information on current exhibits and to scan the correct answers to the trails

Map - View a map of the museum to help you navigate around exhbits

Information- View general information about the museum and its partners

When you scan a QR code and if an artefact is part of a trail then the button will light up purple to let you know what trail it is part of so you can join the trail if you want
