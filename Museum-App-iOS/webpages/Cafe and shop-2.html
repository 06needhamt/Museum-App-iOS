Coffee shop

The coffee shop is situated on the ground floor accessible from the museum atrium. It serves a selection of hot and cold food made by the in house chefs using fresh seasonal produce.

It offers light bites including soup and pasties, alongside a selection of freshly made sandwiches, cakes and pastries, snacks, hot and cold drinks.

Top floor cafe

Situated on the fifth floor this café offers modern affordable food made by the in house chefs using fresh seasonal produce. Take in the roof top views across the city while enjoying a hearty lunch or a light snack. The café serves a selection of hot meals, soup, salads, a selection of freshly made sandwiches, cakes and pastries, snacks, hot and cold drinks.

Opening times:

Daily 10am - 4.45pm (hot food is served from 11.30am to 3.30pm)

Shop

There are two shops located on the ground floor both accessible from the museum atrium.

It stocks a wide range of toys, gifts, books and souvenirs specially tailored to the younger visitor, whether your little ones are interested in roaring dinosaurs, Egyptian mummies, creepy crawly bugs or becoming a space explorer
